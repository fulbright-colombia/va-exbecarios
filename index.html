Fulbright Colombia

By Omar Quintero - Arturo Polo - David Mera

Fulbright Scholarships in Colombia between 1950 - 2015

Tasks

Main Task

Evolution of the scholarships granted by Fulbright in Colombia

To show the evolution of the scholarships granted by the Fulbright Foundation since 1958 in order to identify the growing of them based on the new management and the subscriptions of new agreements

Secondary Tasks


Distribution and diversity of socio-demographic features in the scholarships

To search the distribution and diversity of the socio-demographic features of the Fulbright Foundation scholarships for taking into account aspects such as university, educational level, gender and geographical region where people come from.

Relationships between Colombian and Americans Universities

To find the strength of the relationships between Colombian and Americans universities in order to suggest which should be the better strategy for each agreement.

Insights

  • Before 1978 the foundation starts working with three agreements who are responsible of the 92% of the scholarships granted in this period. The name of these agreements are JWF (BECA J. WILLIAM FULBRIGHT), STUD (BECA ESTUDIANTE ESTADOUNIDENSE) and SCHO (BECA PROFESOR ESTADOUNIDENSE). The bad thing is that it was not recovered the field of study where these scholarships were granted, so it's difficult to stablish what really worked.
  • Between 1979 and 1989, it cames the Human and Social Sciences emphasis. From 1979 till 1989, 25 scholarships average per year were granted. This scenario was driven by the increase of the scholarships granted of some agreements called ATG (AIRWAYS TRAVEL GRANT +600%), FLB-LSP (BECA FULBRIGHT-LASPAU +220%), SIR (BECA PROFESOR COLOMBIANO EN RESIDENCIA +1400%) and CIP (COUNCIL OF INTERNATIONAL PROGRAMS +1000%).
  • Between 1990 and 1996, the engineering scholarships suffered reduction by 73%. The J. WILLIAM FULBRIGHT agreement, who had granted 29 average scholarships per year in the past, during this period only contributed with 5 scholarships average.
  • Since 1997, the foundation have had an education sholarships focus. The new boom is the agreement called BECA ASISTENTE EN LA ENSEÑANZA DEL INGLÉS. Just through this agreement from 2008 till 2015 were granted same amount of scholarships (150) than the number of scholarships granted by all the agreements between 1997 and 2007.
